Quick Omelette

Description

The most simple omelette that you could make. It only takes 5-10 minutes and is delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Sliced green onions
  • Black pepper
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs with the green onions and lay evenly on the pan
  2. Cover slightly in soy sauce + pepper
  3. Fold the omelette into a semicircle
  4. Flip to cook both sides and serve with salt + pepper!

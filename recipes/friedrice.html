Fried Rice

Description

My favorite easy-to-make fried rice (it's not as complicated as the picture above)!

It requires no giant wok or gas stove. Here's how to do it:

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • White rice
  • Peas
  • Diced carrots
  • Sliced green onions
  • Shrimp (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk the two eggs
  2. Pour whisked eggs into pan and scramble until slightly cooked
  3. Store slightly cooked eggs away for later and put rice on the pan
  4. Add the eggs back in and start to fry the shrimp
  5. Add the peas, diced carrots, and sliced green onions after or during cooking
  6. Plate the fried rice and top it with soy sauce or pepper!

Pepper Steak

Description

Recognize this pepper steak? It's one of my favorite recipes, a copy of one of my favorite restaurants!

Ingredients

  • Sliced brisket
  • Sweet corn
  • White rice
  • Sliced green onions
  • Butter
  • Honey soy sauce
  • White onions (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by frying the rice and corn
  2. Add in the butter and cover it with rice
  3. Throw in the sliced brisket and cover everything with honey soy sauce
  4. Mix and fry everything thoroughly
  5. Throw in the sliced green onions and white onions
  6. Serve hot on a cast iron skillet (don't burn yourself!)
